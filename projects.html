Education & dignity

Uniforms and sandals for school children

Many children in rural communities attend school barefoot or in torn, oversized clothing. We provide properly sewn uniforms and durable sandals, working with schools to identify the children who need support most.

  • Encourages regular school attendance
  • Restores confidence and belonging
  • Directs support to vulnerable pupils
